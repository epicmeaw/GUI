League of Legends

This is just a quick rundown on the basics of League


League is a team base game where the objective of the game is the destroy the enemy inhibitor. There are many objectives on the map for you to take advantage of when you are fighting your opponents.


These are some of the few Champions that I have played. Lux is a support who can slow down the enemy and root them in place, and her ult can do a tremendous amount of damage. While Pyke is more movement with an ability to hook the enemy to oneself and a dash that will stun the opponents. My favorite ability is his ult which is an execute that kills the enemy when they reach a certain amount of health and grants gold to your teammate and oneself.

Maps

We have the top, mid, and bot lanes on the map. The jungle is also filled with creatures to kill for exp. I like to play as the support who is in bot lane or the jungle. They are position which is crucial in determining your team's success. This is because they are not the damage dealer but the roles that create an opportunity to engage with the opponents.
